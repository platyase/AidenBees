All About Bees

Why Bees Are Amazing:

Did you know that bees are some of the most important creatures on Earth? Here are some cool facts I’ve learned:

  • Bees pollinate about one-third of the food we eat!
  • Honeybees have five eyes and can see ultraviolet light.
  • A worker bee can only make about 1/12th of a teaspoon of honey in her entire life!
  • The queen bee is the boss of the hive, and she can lay up to 2,000 eggs a day.

How You Can Help Bees

Let’s Work Together to Save the Bees:

Bees need our help! Here are some simple things you can do to make the world a better place for bees:

  • Plant flowers in your garden or on your balcony for bees to visit.
  • Don’t use harmful pesticides or chemicals around your plants.
  • Set out a shallow dish of water with small stones for bees to drink from.
  • Support local beekeepers by buying their honey.
